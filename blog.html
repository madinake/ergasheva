  • The Guide

    Get perfect skin with the Korean 10-step skincare routine. Learn how to double cleanse, exfoliate, and moisturize for a clear, youthful complexion. Say goodbye to blemishes and uneven skin tone and hello to radiant, healthy skin. Follow this ultimate guide for a flawless complexion. .

  • The Famous 10 Steps

    Begin your K-beauty journey with a double cleanse: first, use an oil-based cleanser to remove makeup and oil-based impurities. Follow with a water-based cleanser to whisk away any remaining dirt and sweat. Exfoliate once a week to remove dead skin cells and balance your skin's pH with a toner after cleansing. .

    Next, layer on an essence to hydrate and protect your skin. Address specific skin concerns with treatments, serums, or boosters. Treat your skin to a sheet mask once a week for an extra boost of hydration. Care for the delicate skin around your eyes with an eye cream, and seal in all the goodness with a moisturizer. Finally, finish with sun protection (SPF) to shield your skin from damage.

  • Get your Glossy Skin

    The 10-step Korean skincare routine is famous for its complexity and results. But the beauty of this routine is you don't need to be do every step every day. The full 10 steps should happen once or twice a week, maximum. Every other day, you can make it a 5 or 7-step Korean skincare routine, relative to your skin needs. Following this alongside K-beauty products will set you on the road to getting the skin you want. .
